Ecosystem Laws

Operational Protocols

Unique Identity

NEXRA permits exactly one account per physical device and IP address. System AI scans for clones 24/7. Violators face instant permanent suspension.

Node Integrity

Tasks must be processed with real human interaction. Automation scripts, VPNs, or headless browsers are detected as a protocol breach. Verified work only.

Settlement Window

Withdrawal audits are manual. The standard queue time is 2 to 3 hours. Requests during bank holidays may extend slightly. Patience is mandatory.

Referral Conduct

Partners are rewarded for bringing Active, Verified Members. Systematic creation of fake pending referrals to manipulate team stats will result in a VIP demotion.
