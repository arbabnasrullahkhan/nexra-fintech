Privacy Protocols

AES-256 Cloud Encryption

NEXRA uses military-grade encryption to secure your Wallet IDs, Account Numbers, and login credentials. Your data is fragmented and stored across decentralized technical nodes.

  • Identity Data: We collect only necessary data (Email, Number) to verify your account status and prevent fraud.
  • No Third-Party Access: NEXRA does not sell or share your activity logs with advertisers. Your earning history is 100% private.
  • IP Mapping: We use IP addresses solely to protect the community from bot attacks and multi-account abuse.
  • Data Control: Users can request account deactivation and data purging via the Concierge Support channel.
